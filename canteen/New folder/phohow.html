1.Link Your MaceWallet With Your Bank Account.


You can link your MaceWallet with your Bank Account through following steps...

a. Select "Link/Unlink Your Bank Account" from Banking Tab.
b. Enter your Bank Account Details and Proceed.


c. Enter the OTP which was sent to your registered mobile number.
d. Continue and Recharge your MaceWallet.

2.Recharge Your MaceWallet.

You can Recharge Your MaceWallet by transfering funds from your bank account through following steps...(Please make sure that your MaceWallet is linked to your Bank Account)

a. Select "Recharge your MaceWallet" from Banking Tab.
b. Enter the amount to recharge.


c. Click on Proceed Button.
d. Enter the OTP which has been sent to your registered mobile number.
5.Click Continue.

3.WithDraw Cash From Your MaceWallet.


You can Withdraw money from your MaceWallet through following steps...

a. Select "Withdraw Money From MaceWallet" from Banking Tab.
b. Enter the amount to Withdraw.


c. Click Confirm to Proceed.

4.Change Your Password.


You can change your password through following steps...

a. Select "Account" from the top right corner.


b. Select "Change Your Password".


c. Enter your old password and new password.


d.Click Confirm.

5.Lock your Barcode Transactions.


You can block your Barcode incase you lost your ID Card. You can do this by following these steps...

a. Select "Account" from the top right corner.


b. Select "Barcode Lock".


c. Select Lock and Confirm.
